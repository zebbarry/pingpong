 --------- PONG ----------
 START: Board is ready to start a game when the words 'PONG' are scrolling across the screen. Press center navswtich button to begin a game with a nearby board (in a ready-to-start state) to initiate a game with that board.

 RULES: Move your paddle to prevent the ball from touching your end. If the ball touches your end, your opponent gains a point, and the current score is displayed in the format 'xy', x = your score, y = opponent's score.

 CONTROL:   - Center navswitch  - Start Game
            - Navswitch left    - Paddle Right
            - Navswitch right   - Paddle Left
